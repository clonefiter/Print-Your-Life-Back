# Print-Your-Life-Back

Print Your Life Back is a research project meant to explore the benefits of 3D printing concrete housing during a disaster scenario. Our original research question was “In what ways are robot arm concrete 3D printers more efficient at constructing houses as opposed to traditional housing?”

3D printing of concrete has made many recent breakthroughs, introducing easier ways of using it for housing purposes. Our main focus on this research project was to look at the efficacy of using a portable robotic arm for expedited "printing" of houses. This relates to our original question, and allows us to explore the engineering and software that goes into a construction technology such as this.
